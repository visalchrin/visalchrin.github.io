How to Protect Yourself From Covid-19

1. How do I wash my hands properly?

  • Step 1: Wet hands with running water
  • Step 2: Apply enough soap to cover wet hands
  • Step 3: Scrub all surfaces of the hands – including back of hands, between fingers and under nails – for at least 20 seconds
  • Step 4: Rinse thoroughly with running water
  • Step 5: Dry hands with a clean cloth or single-use towel

2. How do I wear masks properly?

  • Step 1: Clean your hands before you put your mask on, as well as before and after you take it off, and after you touch it at any time.
  • Step 2: Make sure it covers both your nose, mouth and chin.
  • Step 3: When you take off a mask, store it in a clean plastic bag, and every day either wash it if it’s a fabric mask, or dispose of a medical mask in a trash bin.
  • Step 4: Don’t use masks with valves.

3. How to properly keep your distance?

  • Step 1: Avoid close contact
  • Step 2: Meet the same people
  • Step 3: Keep your distance
  • Step 4: Time your trips outside
